## 1. Business Context

GTV is a **visa intelligence + destination discovery platform** operating at the intersection of two massive markets:

| Layer                   | What It Does                                                  |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Visa Intelligence**   | Which passport gets in where, on what terms, and how to apply |
| **Destination Content** | Inspiring and guiding travelers after they know they can go   |

The database reveals the full product: `visa_eligibility`, `visa_types`, `countries`, multilingual destination hubs at `/en/d/[country]`, and a blog in 8 languages. This is not a generic travel blog — it is a **decision-support engine** for international travelers, particularly from high-travel, visa-complex markets.

**Core customer question (their actual words):**

> _"هل أحتاج تأشيرة؟" / "Do I need a visa?" / "Can I travel to X with my Y passport?"_

This is the most valuable search query this website can own.

## 4. SEO + AI-SEO Framework

### 4.1 Traditional SEO: Keyword Targeting by Buyer Stage

| Stage             | Modifier Pattern                        | Example                                          | Priority |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | -------- |
| **Awareness**     | "do I need a visa for"                  | "do I need a visa for Japan with Saudi passport" | 🔴 #1    |
| **Consideration** | "how to apply for" / "requirements for" | "how to apply for UK visa from India"            | 🔴 #1    |
| **Decision**      | "visa checklist" / "documents needed"   | "Japan visa documents checklist for Egyptians"   | 🟠 #2    |
| **Inspiration**   | "best time to visit" / "is X worth it"  | "is Saudi Arabia worth visiting 2025"            | 🟡 #3    |
| **Comparison**    | "vs" / "or" / "difference between"      | "Dubai vs Bali for family vacation"              | 🟡 #3    |

### 4.2 Keyword Modifiers to Mine

```
Awareness:    what is, how to, guide to, can I travel to, do I need
Consideration: best, top, requirements, documents, checklist, apply for
Decision:     cost, fee, processing time, how long, approval rate
Inspiration:  worth visiting, best time, how many days, itinerary
Comparison:   vs, or, difference, better, alternative
```

### 4.3 AI-SEO (LLM Discovery) — 2025–2026 Priority

AI assistants are becoming the first point of contact for travel planning. To appear in AI answers:

#### Rule 1: Answer in Sentence One

AI models extract and cite the first clear answer they find.

| ❌ Don't                                                             | ✅ Do                                                                      |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| "Japan is a beautiful country that many people dream of visiting..." | "Saudi passport holders can visit Japan without a visa for up to 90 days." |

#### Rule 2: Entity Consistency Across Languages

GTV publishes in 8 languages — a massive AI-SEO signal. When Arabic, French, Spanish, and English versions agree on facts, AI models learn to trust GTV as an authoritative source.

#### Rule 3: Structured Data Requirements

| Schema Type            | Where                   | Status                  |
| ---------------------- | ----------------------- | ----------------------- |
| FAQ schema             | All blog articles       | ✅ Implemented (Arabic) |
| HowTo schema           | Visa application guides | 🔴 Missing              |
| BreadcrumbList         | Hub/spoke navigation    | 🔴 Missing              |
| Article + dateModified | All articles            | 🟡 Partial              |

#### Rule 4: Cite Official Sources

AI systems prefer content that references official sources. Every visa article must link to:

- `mofa.go.jp` (Japan)
- `canada.ca/en/immigration` (Canada)
- `gov.uk/standard-visitor-visa` (UK)
- `travel.state.gov` (USA)
- `ec.europa.eu` (Schengen)
- `visa.visitsaudi.com` (Saudi)

#### Rule 5: The "GTV Says" Test

Write content confidently enough that an AI would cite: _"According to GTV, Saudi citizens can enter Japan visa-free..."_ This requires authoritative, verified, specific claims — not hedged generalizations.

#### Rule 6: Comprehensive Topic Coverage

The FAQ sections at the bottom of every article are the right approach. Extend this pattern site-wide. AI models favor pages that answer all related questions in one place.

## 9. The Single Biggest Opportunity

### The Halal Travel Hub

**Halal travel is the most underserved, highest-value content category in the travel space right now.**

| Signal               | Data                                                          |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Addressable audience | 1.8 billion Muslims globally                                  |
| Market size          | $220B Muslim travel spend (2023), projected $300B by 2026     |
| Competition          | Near-zero quality Arabic OR English-language content          |
| GTV advantage        | Arabic-language infrastructure already built                  |
| Content scale        | Every destination has a "halal version" waiting to be written |

A **Halal Travel Hub** — with guides for Japan, South Korea, Europe, Bali, and beyond, written in Arabic, French, and English, with halal certification details, prayer facility locations, and restaurant data — would be a category-defining move with essentially no direct competition at quality.

**This is GTV's version of what NomadList did for remote work, or what The Points Guy did for credit card travel: own a specific identity in the travel space before anyone else does.**

## Appendix B: Article Quality Checklist

Every article published must satisfy:

- [ ] Primary keyword appears in title (≤65 chars)
- [ ] Primary keyword appears in first sentence of body
- [ ] Meta description is 120–160 chars with primary keyword
- [ ] At least one H2 contains primary keyword variant
- [ ] FAQ section with ≥4 H3 questions (Google rich results)
- [ ] At least one comparison table where applicable
- [ ] Internal link to relevant destination hub (`/en/d/[country]`)
- [ ] Internal link to at least 2 related blog articles
- [ ] Authority outbound link to official government source (visa articles)
- [ ] Content ≥900 words (4,000+ characters)
- [ ] `dateModified` in article schema (recency signal for AI)
