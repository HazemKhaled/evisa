- **Cost efficiency**: Better flight deals and shared travel costs
- **Cultural diversity**: Experience different cultures in one journey

## Cultural Considerations

### Germany

- **Language**: German (English widely spoken)
- **Tipping**: Round up or 10%
- **Customs**: Punctuality highly valued

### United Kingdom

- **Language**: English
- **Tipping**: 10-15% in restaurants
- **Customs**: Queuing culture, politeness

### United Arab Emirates

- **Language**: Arabic (English widespread)
- **Tipping**: 10-15% standard
- **Customs**: Modest dress, respect for local traditions
