## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does visa processing take?

Processing times vary by destination and visa type. Tourist visas typically take 1-4 weeks, while work or study visas may take 2-8 weeks. We'll provide specific timelines based on your situation.

### What documents do I need?

Required documents depend on your destination and visa type. Common requirements include:
- Valid passport
- Passport photos
- Financial proof
- Travel itinerary
- Accommodation details

### Can you help with emergency travel?

Yes! We offer expedited services for urgent travel situations. Contact us immediately if you need to travel within 48-72 hours.

### Do you guarantee visa approval?

While we cannot guarantee approval (as that's decided by government authorities), we ensure your application is complete, accurate, and properly prepared to maximize your chances of success.
